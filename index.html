Reporting
Securely report crimes with confidence using our blockchain-based system.

What we are doing?

We are developing a portal which is as an extension to GoI's current report recording and management. It is a solution for people by people, and will be powered by government.

What makes us different?

With our community driven solution, we make crime reporting more transperent, tamper proof and safe. Transperent, such that everyone in community can view incident reports and report complaints on our public blockchain. Once hashed and added to the blockchain, incident reports cannot be modified making our system tamper proof. To protect our reporters, we bring a system such that their identites will be kept anonymous unless otherwise becomes extremely necessary.
